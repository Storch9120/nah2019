Petting the Python

Python Workshop

In the world of modern technology, computers have become so accessible that, as a tool, almost anyone on Planet Earth can benefit from it. Thus an essential tool that can realise an idea and make a change to the world is programming. Python seems to be the most progressively used programming language right now, as it is very intuitive, reads easily, and has an incredible amount of community support and documentation. It is used every day in every field, from marine biology to football management. In addition, (on a more practical note), CBSE has recently changed its high school syllabus from C++ to Python. Thus, programming has cemented itself as an important tool to be familiar with in the 21st century.

Target Audience

This workshop is for students who are interested in knowing what programming is and how to get started building projects of their own. Even though it's not essential to know how to program prior to attending this workshop, if you have (for example Java), it will definitely help. In addition, some resources have been added if you wish to explore Python on your own. If you are interested or have doubts regarding the workshop or topics discussed, please email us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Detective S&P(ikachu)

Finance Workshop

Stock prices of Melon Musk's company Tezla has recently risen after the merger with its once rival, Bapple Inc., as many financial gurus are expecting this deal to be very profitable to investors in the near future. A dusty old computer somewhere in the large datacenters right next to Wall Street, however, detected unusual buying habits of one of the Bapple's chairperson's trusted advisors, Mr. J.P. Murugan, right before the merger. It seems just two days before the deal was announced, Mr. Murugan bought a hefty stake in Tezla stocks. Some folks in the financial protection department of the city suspect fraudulent activity and have called upon you to take a look. It's your job now to find out whether indeed this was just a coincidence, or if there's something else lurking in the background.

Breakthroughs in Biology from a Bioinformatic Perspective

Biology Workshop

All this will be understood via hands-on bioinformatic analysis and thorough discussion. Bioinformatics is a field at the intersection between biology and statistics and leverages a number of computer software programs in practice. It is not only very captivating but also very useful for carrying out general biological research. Bioinformatic tools used would help in visualisation of proteins, structural comparison of proteins and gene sequence comparisons.
