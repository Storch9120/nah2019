Our Events

Hackathonesque

The idea is to collaboratively code in an extreme manner, to start from scratch and end with a working prototype. It’s a competition, where teams of coders and ideators come together to design & build a product, a software project. In teams of 2-6, you come up with ideas and attempt to code a rudimentary app to solve real-life problems that ae pressing concerns in the current world and then at the end of 30 hours, present the idea to a panel of distinguished judges who will decide who becomes the next potential Mark Zuckerberg.

Not A Quiz

Debate

In an age where a far-reaching tech revolution is underway, rapid developments in technologies such as genetic-engineering and artificial Intelligence, as well as the quest for potential ground-breaking leaps in quantum computing and nuclear fission as a source of energy will bring about significant changes to our economies, societies, politics and our lives as a whole. Hence, it is becoming increasingly important to hold elaborate discussions on relevant issues. ‘Not a Quiz’ entails highly exciting competitive debates on pressing concerns in which participants can display their debating and articulation skills.

Cipher Quest

Treasure Hunt

Cipher Quest is an in-campus Treasure Hunt competition where students work together to solve a given set of questions using logic, team-work and use of online resources. Every question has a designated location assigned in the school. After solving a particular question, the participating team is provided with a clue to the location of the next question. The first team to solve all the questions wins.

Workshops

Finance, Python, Biology & many more

This series of workshops are designed to introduce students to the world of technology in a non-technological way - an introduction to the wide uses of computers in pure maths, genetics, and economics. In addition, there will be a two-hour intensive workshop that will let the student grasp coding concepts and learn Python in a hands-on way. More details to follow.

Escape Room

What if you and your friends were eminent personalities of the country and had been kidnapped by a militant group, The Emancipators, to blackmail the president for your release? Little would they know about your skills to execute an escape plan before the president would make a compromise! Well, Not A Hackathon gives you a chance to experience this in real life. Make teams of five and get ready feel the adrenaline rush like never before, an exciting 15 minutes that will challenge all your senses! Find clues, analyse, and solve the puzzles to escape in time. Do you think you can do it?
